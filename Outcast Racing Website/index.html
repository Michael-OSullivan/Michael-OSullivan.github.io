Here is our top 5 list of MTB spots in Ireland

1. The GAP

This would be our top pick due to the variety of trails and the well managed grading. There is a trail to suit anyone from beginner to pro, which guarantees a great day out for everyone.They offer an efficient uplift service to take the effort out of your trip. There is ample amount of space for lunch at the GAP. The food is top class! Overall we are rating our Trip 10/10 and we will be back!

2. Tickknock

This is the home of progression. You are guaranteed to test your self from begineer to expert on these trails. Biking.ie offers a bike rental service which is great for bringing your friends to come ride. Overall this is where the hobby started for most and Ticknock is guaranteed to have you hooked!
